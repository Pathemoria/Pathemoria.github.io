Crime & Punishment

F. Dostoevsky

My favorite book, actually! I remember, that it was also my very first Dostoevsky book that I had ever read!

Raskolnikov & Sonya continue to be characters that I am deeply in love with, as well as what they represent as written characters, often during my writing, I continue to think of them, as if, what would they do if they were here, in this scene that I myself had written.
